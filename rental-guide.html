GUIDE

Insurance & Rental Information

Everything you need to know before and during your rental.

  • Collision Damage Waiver (CDW) is included in every rental.
  • Third-party liability insurance is included as standard.
  • Excess applies: €300 deductible in the event of vehicle damage.
  • Upgrade to Super CDW to reduce your excess to €0.
  • Super CDW also covers tyre, windscreen and underbody damage.
  • Personal belongings and valuables are not covered.
  • Off-road and beach driving voids all insurance coverage.
  • Valid driving licence required, held for a minimum of 1 year.
  • Minimum driver age: 21 years.
  • Maximum driver age: 75 years.
  • Drivers aged 21–24: a young driver surcharge applies per day.
  • Non-EU licence holders must present an International Driving Permit.
  • A valid credit card in the main driver's name is required at pickup.
  • Up to 3 additional authorised drivers can be added to the agreement.
  • No smoking inside any vehicle — a €80 cleaning fee will apply.
  • Pets are welcome with prior notice; a cleaning fee may apply.
  • Off-road, beach and unpaved-road driving is strictly prohibited.
  • All vehicles must remain within the borders of Greece at all times.
  • Report any accident, incident or damage to us immediately.
  • Do not attempt to repair the vehicle yourself — call us first.
  • Seat belts must be worn by all passengers at all times by law.
  • All vehicles are collected with a full tank of fuel.
  • The vehicle must be returned with a full tank.
  • Failure to return full: refuelling charge + €25 service fee.
  • The fuel type (petrol or diesel) is shown on the dashboard sticker.
  • Always use the correct fuel — misfuelling is not covered by insurance.
  • We do not offer a prepaid fuel option.
  • Contact us immediately: +30 22420 92265 or WhatsApp +30 694 477 1738.
  • Do not move the vehicle unless it is blocking traffic and it is safe to do so.
  • Call the police (100) for any collision involving another vehicle or personal injury.
  • Take photographs of all vehicles, damage, and the surrounding area.
  • Obtain the other driver's name, address, licence plate, and insurer.
  • Do not admit fault or sign any statement at the scene.
  • Complete the European Accident Report form found in the glove box.
  • Pull over safely, apply the handbrake, and switch on your hazard lights.
  • The spare wheel and tool kit are located in the vehicle's boot.
  • Contact us before changing the tyre — we can arrange assistance at no extra charge.
  • Do not continue driving on a flat; doing so causes damage to the wheel rim.
  • Standard CDW does not cover tyre damage; Super CDW includes tyre protection.
  • Return the flat tyre with the vehicle so we can assess the cause of the puncture.
  • Contact us immediately if keys are lost, stolen, or locked inside the vehicle.
  • Key replacement costs from €150, depending on the vehicle model.
  • Key loss is not covered by CDW or Super CDW insurance.
  • If keys are stolen, file a police report and provide us with a copy.
  • A spare key is held at our office for emergency use during opening hours.
  • Never leave the vehicle unlocked or keys unattended in a public place.
  • Return the vehicle to our office at 12 Pavlou Mela, Kos Town.
  • The return date and time are stated in your rental agreement.
  • Late returns beyond a 1-hour grace period incur an additional day's rental charge.
  • The vehicle will be inspected jointly at the time of return.
  • The fuel tank must be full; a refuelling charge plus a €25 service fee applies otherwise.
  • Remove all personal belongings before handing over the keys.
  • For after-hours returns, please contact us in advance to arrange a key drop-off.
  • Our 24-hour emergency line: +30 694 477 1738 (WhatsApp / call)
  • Office direct line: +30 22420 92265
  • European emergency number: 112 (police, fire, ambulance)
  • Greek Police: 100
  • Roadside assistance (ELPA): 10400
  • Kos General Hospital: +30 224 202 2300
  • Can I pay by debit card? A credit card in the main driver's name is required as a security deposit; debit cards are accepted for the rental balance.
  • Is there a mileage limit? No — all rentals include unlimited mileage within Greece.
  • Can I drive to Turkey? Vehicles may not leave Greek territory under any circumstances.
  • What if I return the car late? Returns beyond 1 hour past the agreed time incur an extra day's charge.
  • Can I add an additional driver later? Yes — additional drivers must be added at our office and signed onto the agreement before driving.
  • Are child seats available? Child and baby seats are available on request at a small daily fee.
